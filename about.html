Welcome to

Morodok is a traditional Khmer restaurant devoted to preserving and sharing Cambodia’s cultural heritage through authentic cuisine. We believe that food is not just nourishment but a story passed down through generations. Join us for a culinary journey rich in flavor, tradition, and warm hospitality.

Alton Brown

Executive Chef

Biography

Chef Alton Brown has over 15 years of culinary experience, specializing in modern American cuisine with a focus on farm-to-table ingredients. He graduated from the Culinary Institute of America and has worked in renowned restaurants across the country.

Specialties

• Modern American Cuisine
• Farm-to-Table Cooking
• Molecular Gastronomy
• Wine Pairing

Awards & Recognition

  • James Beard Award Nominee (2020, 2021)
  • Best Chef Southeast - Food & Wine Magazine (2019)
  • Michelin Star Restaurant Experience
  • Featured in Top Chef Masters
